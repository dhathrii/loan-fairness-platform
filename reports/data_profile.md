# Responsible Lending Decision System

## Dataset

German Credit Dataset

## Business Problem

Predict whether a loan applicant is high-risk or low-risk.

## Target

Credit Risk

## Fairness Attributes

- Age
- Personal Status Sex
- Foreign Worker

## Feature Categories

### Financial
- Status
- Savings
- Amount

### Employment
- Employment Duration
- Job

### Creditworthiness
- Credit History
- Number Credits

### Personal
- Age
- Housing
- Residence
